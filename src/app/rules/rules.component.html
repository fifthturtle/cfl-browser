SCORING: Of the 18 players/defenses on your team, the following players are scored each week: The top 2 QB’s, top 3 RBs & 3 WRs, and your 1 top kicker and 1 top defense.

MONEY: The weekly high score winners get $20. In addition, the highest one week score will get $40 and the CFL season total points leader will now get $40. The Top 5 teams win money. 1st 30%, 2nd 20%, 3rd 12%, 4th 8%, 5th 6%. The three division winners win money (8-8-8%).

TIEBREAKERS: In the event of a tie game, the winner will be determined by tallying the top score at each position.
